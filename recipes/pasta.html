Spaghetti


Aglio e olio is one of the best basic pasta recipes ever! A simple yet delicious Italian pasta dish that requires olive oil, garlic, and chili flakes. Simple is best.

Ingredients:

  • Regular or whole wheat spaghetti
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Fresh or dried chili flakes
  • Salt
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Parsley (optional)
